What changed structurally:
  • Dropped 2 columns — Guest and Source/Age moved into the subtitle line under each request. Saves ~280px of horizontal space.
  • Status column always full-text — "Acknowledged" not "ACKNOWLEDC". Progress dots + step text below the pill.
  • Action column always visible — never clipped. Buttons are workflow-aware:
    • new Airport → "✓ Schedule pickup"
    • new Mini-shop → "✓ Approve" "✕"
    • acknowledged Housekeeping → "→ Assign" "✓ Done"
    • driver_assigned Transport → "✓ Mark in transit"
  • Click anywhere on a row still opens the detail drawer. Action buttons don't trigger that — they fire the workflow transition directly.
  • Touch-friendly buttons — 32px tall, easy to tap on mobile or tablet at reception.
