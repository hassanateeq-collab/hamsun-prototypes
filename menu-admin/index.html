Kitchen & Slack notifications What happens when an order is placed

→When an order is confirmed by the guest

  • Slack KOT fires immediately to #kitchen-fsl (per slot, one ticket: room, time, location, num covers, per-person items + modifiers + sides + drinks)
  • WhatsApp confirmation sent to the room's guest service group
  • Order locks 40 min before slot start — guest can edit until then
  • Charge for extras (if any) auto-posted to the folio via post-charge

→Daily kitchen summary

  • 06:30 AM Slack message to #kitchen-fsl aggregating every order for the morning
  • Grouped by time slot — cooks see prep counts at a glance
  • Per-room breakdown: room, covers, location, items

→Status updates (optional)

  • Preparing: Slack-button click → "Your breakfast is being prepared 👨‍🍳" goes to room group
  • Ready: Slack-button click → "Your breakfast is ready and on its way 🛎️"
  • Delivered: Slack-button click → "Enjoy your breakfast! 🌅"
  • Disable any of these if you don't want guests notified at every stage

Plating & decoration Per-dish presentation notes for kitchen

→How presentation flows

  • Each main course can carry plating notes (added in its edit dialog)
  • Notes appear on the Slack KOT and the daily summary so the kitchen sees them at prep time
  • Two layers of notes:
    • Prep notes: "Roll fresh, soft cook" — for the cook
    • Plating notes: "Serve on warm plate, garnish with coriander" — for the line / steward
  • In-room deliveries also get a "Tray setup" note (linen, condiment caddy, water carafe) — global default below, override per dish if needed
